{% trans "Risk Data Portal" %}

{% blocktrans %}

The Risk Data Portal of Thessaloniki is an online geospatial data catalog where registered data owners can share geospatial data, metadata and related documents as open data or to a limited group of registered users, and where any user can preview and download geospatial data, metadata and related documents.

{% endblocktrans %} {% blocktrans %}

The Risk Data Portal is intended to help the community of risk stakeholders to improve the Resilience of the city of Thessaloniki.

{% endblocktrans %} {% blocktrans %}

The Risk Data Portal is a project initiated by the Urban Resilience Initiative for Thessaloniki in collaboration with the World Bank. The website is jointly managed by the GIS and the eGov departments of the municipality.

{% endblocktrans %} {% blocktrans %}

The Risk Data Portal, based on the open source software GeoNode, has been deployed with technical support from Open Knowledge Greece and EOFarm P.C..
